How does AI learn?
Training an AI is all about repetition. The AI reads a sentence, tries to predict the next word, checks how wrong it was, then nudges its internal numbers slightly in the right direction. After doing this billions of times across terabytes of text, it gets very good at predicting words — and that's essentially what "intelligence" is in an LLM.
