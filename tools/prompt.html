💡 The core idea: AI is not magic — it responds exactly to how you ask. "Write a poem" gives generic results. "Write a 4-line rhyming poem about coffee from the perspective of someone who forgot their morning cup" gives something great. Prompt engineering is the skill of writing clear, specific instructions that get you the output you actually want.
Compare prompts and see the difference
Prompt Engineering
A prompt is your instruction to the AI. The more specific and structured your prompt, the better the output. The difference between a bad and a great AI response is usually the quality of the prompt, not the AI model. Learning to write good prompts is one of the most valuable skills you can develop when working with AI.
