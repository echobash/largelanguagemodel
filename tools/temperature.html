What is Temperature?
Imagine temperature like a dial on a slot machine. At 0, the machine always gives the same result — safe and predictable. At 2.0, it spins wildly — very creative but sometimes nonsensical. For coding tasks, use low temperature (0.2). For creative writing, use higher temperature (0.8-1.0).
